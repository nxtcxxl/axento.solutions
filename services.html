Services

We help growing teams design a lean operating system—map processes, remove bottlenecks, automate routine, and make data drive decisions.

Core Services

Operations Audit

Deep-dive review of how your organization works today.

  • Interviews with founders/leads, team surveys, meeting shadowing
  • Process mapping & bottleneck analysis
  • Findings report with prioritized recommendations

Operating Model & Implementation

Design the structure, roles, and rituals that keep execution predictable.

  • Governance & decision rights, RACI / role clarity
  • Process & SOP drafting, OKRs / KPIs, cadence setup
  • Change enablement and hands-on rollout

Mentoring & Advisory

On‑call guidance for founders and managers.

  • 1:1 sessions or monthly subscription
  • Support for hiring, planning, and prioritization
  • Rapid response to operational blockers

Workflow Automation

Custom tools and integrations to eliminate repetitive work.

  • Low‑code/high‑code automations (Notion, Google, Slack, custom)
  • Request/approval flows, onboarding checklists, handoff guards
  • Reliability first: observability and SLA mindset

Data & Metrics

Turn activity into insight with a simple measurement stack.

  • KPI trees; dashboards for delivery, quality, and finance
  • Lightweight data collection and analytics instrumentation
  • Review rhythms and decision templates

Platform (in development)

Internal platform for tracking metrics, onboarding, and load control.

  • Buyout or SaaS subscription
  • Built from real client patterns
  • Private pilots available
