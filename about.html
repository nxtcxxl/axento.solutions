About aXento.solutions

We help small tech companies structure operations, optimize processes, and build data-driven workflows that scale. Our team combines consulting discipline with product thinking to bring clarity and momentum to your business.

Our Story

Founded in Lviv, Ukraine, aXento.solutions started with a simple goal: make operations simple and measurable. We partner with startups and SMBs to build lean processes, automate repetitive work, and set up the right analytics from day one.

We work as a compact unit of specialists—operations, automation, and data—so you can move faster without growing overhead.
